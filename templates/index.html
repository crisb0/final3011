Who can use TrackPants?

Here's who we can think of, but surely creative people will surprise us.

Content Creators

Track your progress as an influencer. Strive to achieve the goals you've set for social engagement and reach.

Brands

Engage with your product users. Connect to their opinions to your work and view this in relation to your stocks.

A platform to improve your company's performance

TrackPants brings you the data you'll need and the structure to utilise this data, without a learning curve. It aims to strengthen the connection you have with your users by tracking the performance of your campaigns and analysing what works for your company.

Intuitive representation of your campaign data

TrackPants presents the data from each company campagin in a format that allows for easy inferrence and extraction of information.
